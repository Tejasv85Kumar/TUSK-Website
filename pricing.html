FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time?⌄
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the end of your current billing cycle.
Is there a free trial available?⌄
We offer a 14-day free trial on the Professional plan with no credit card required. Contact our sales team to arrange an Enterprise proof-of-concept.
What happens if I exceed my transaction limit?⌄
If you exceed your monthly transaction limit, overage charges apply at $0.002 per transaction. We notify you at 80% and 95% usage so you can upgrade proactively.
Do you offer custom pricing for very large institutions?⌄
Absolutely. For institutions processing 1M+ transactions per month, we offer custom enterprise agreements with volume discounts, SLA guarantees, and dedicated infrastructure.
What support channels are included?⌄
Starter includes email support (Mon–Fri 9AM–6PM IST). Professional and Enterprise include 24/7 priority phone and chat support, plus a dedicated Slack channel for Enterprise.
Is data encrypted and compliant?⌄
Yes. All data is encrypted with AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. TUSK AI is PCI-DSS Level 1, SOC 2 Type II, and ISO 27001 certified.
