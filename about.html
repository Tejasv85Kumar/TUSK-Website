What is TUSK AI?

Built for the Modern Bank

TUSK AI is an enterprise-grade FinTech platform that combines cutting-edge machine learning with real-time behavioral analytics to protect financial institutions and their customers from fraud.

Our platform processes millions of transactions per day, learning and adapting continuously to emerging fraud patterns — while providing intelligent banking assistance through our RAGFlow-powered conversational AI.

From small credit unions to global banks, TUSK AI scales effortlessly to meet the demands of the modern financial landscape.

Our Journey

From Inception to Innovation

2023 — Inception
TUSK AI Founded
Started with a small team of ML engineers and banking domain experts in Kanpur, India, with a vision to democratize enterprise-grade fraud detection.
2023 Q3 — First Product
Fraud Detection Engine v1.0
Launched our first ML model with 92% accuracy, securing 3 pilot banks and processing over 10,000 daily transactions.
2024 Q1 — Scale
Banking Assistant Launch
Integrated RAGFlow to power our conversational AI assistant. Onboarded 50+ financial institutions and crossed 1 million transactions secured.
2024 Q4 — Enterprise
Enterprise Platform v2.0
Achieved 99.7% detection accuracy with LSTM ensemble models. SOC 2 Type II and PCI-DSS certifications obtained. $50M+ fraud prevented.
2025+ — Future
Global Expansion & Innovation
Expanding to 20+ countries, launching mobile SDK, federated learning for privacy-preserving model training, and open API marketplace.
